About Me

Hey there, I'm Mackenzie. I am a mom of two amazing kiddos, a creative entrepreneur, and am genuinely passionate about capturing my client's beautiful moments. It all started a little over a decade ago when I picked up my old Canon camera and began taking pictures of friends and family. It has grown from a hobby to something I am lucky enough to call a career. Since 2016, I have grown into an experienced photography professional. Whether capturing weddings, fun family moments, or celebrating graduations, I am here for your authentic and timeless moments.


A couple fun facts about me: I love hiking and exploring nature any chance I get. I am an Enneagram 9. I have been married to the love of my life for a little under 10 years and he is my best friend. I love spicy food. I promise to make you laugh and make a fool out of myself to ensure the best smiles are being captured


If you're interested in my work, check out my portfolio!

What I bring to the table

  • High resolution images all delivered onto an online downloadable gallery.
  • Your files will be store multiple times on at least two different locations.
  • All images are edited with care and will not be severely altered or changed in the process.
  • I will fight for genuine laughter/expressions even if I need to make a fool of myself.
  • I work with client's budgets and am always honest in my pricing. There are never any added or hidden fees.
